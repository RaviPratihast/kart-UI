Rating

Rating Component helps capture the user feedback in form of rating. The greater the number of people leaving feedback, the more reliable a star rating is likely to be as an indicator of quality. It can be in form of visual starts. Here we have used font awesome for the icon and used classes 'rating', 'rating-checked' and 'rating-unchecked' for the different variations of ratings.
